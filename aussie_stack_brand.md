**Why it ranks**:

- Includes upload cadence, which YouTube weights as an "active channel" signal.
- Clear positioning vs adjacent niches (FIRE, financial-advisor channels) — helps YouTube cluster similar viewers correctly.
- Compliance disclaimer is mandatory under ASIC AFSL rules for any AU finance content.
